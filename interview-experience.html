Overall experience

Preparation tips

  • Focused deeply on Google-tagged problems, especially Strings, Graphs and Heap.
  • Practised mock interviews to get better at thinking and coding under 60-minute constraints.
  • Consistency mattered more than intensity.
  • Solving one high-quality problem daily helped far more than last-minute cramming.

General advice

  • Google interviews are fast-paced and time-bound, so practise with that environment in mind.
  • Don't underestimate edge-case discussions, dry runs, and being able to explain your solution with confidence.
